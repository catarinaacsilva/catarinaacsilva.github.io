Biography

I am an Invited Assistant Professor at the ESTGA department of the University of Aveiro and a Post-Doctoral Researcher affiliated with the Institute of Electronics and Informatics Engineering of Aveiro (IEETA).

Holding a Ph.D. in Computer Science with a specialization in Privacy and Information Security, my research focuses on the development of privacy quantification models, cybersecurity, and the proactive integration of security within the software development lifecycle (DevPrivOps). Throughout my academic career, I have sought to combine regulatory compliance and AI ethics with the design of secure and resilient communication architectures, particularly in Internet of Things (IoT) and 6G network environments.
